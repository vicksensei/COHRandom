55 - 63:  Mastermind  (Mastermind Primary / Defender Primary)

64 - 72:  Scrapper  (Scrapper Primary / Scrapper Secondary)

73 - 81:  Sentinel  (Blaster Primary / Scrapper Secondary)

82 - 90:  Stalker  (Stalker Primary / Scrapper Secondary)

90 - 00:  Tanker  (Tank Primary / Tank Secondary)
